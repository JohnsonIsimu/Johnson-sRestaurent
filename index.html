A tradition of culinary excellence

At Johnson's Restaurant, we believe that dining is more than just eating; it's an experience. Our story began with a passion for bringing people together over beautifully crafted meals.

  • We source the freshest local ingredients to create dishes that sing with flavor.
  • Our team is dedicated to providing warm, attentive service that makes every guest feel like family.
  • From our open kitchen to your table, we ensure every detail, from presentation to taste, is perfect.

Come join us for a meal, a celebration, or a quiet evening. We're here to make it memorable.

Herb-Crusted Grilled Salmon

Fresh Atlantic salmon fillet, grilled to perfection with a medley of garden herbs.

Served with a light lemon butter sauce, seasonal vegetables, and your choice of garlic mashed potatoes or steamed rice. A healthy and flavorful choice.

Creamy Truffle Risotto

Arborio rice slowly cooked to a creamy consistency, infused with the aromatic flavor of black truffle.

Finished with a generous shaving of parmesan cheese and a drizzle of truffle oil. A luxurious vegetarian delight.

Classic Beef Wellington

A tender beef fillet coated with mushroom duxelles and prosciutto, baked in a golden puff pastry.

This show-stopping dish is served with a rich red wine demi-glace and roasted root vegetables. Perfect for a special occasion.

Traditional Seafood Paella

A generous portion of saffron-infused Spanish rice loaded with fresh shrimp, mussels, clams, and calamari.

Cooked in a traditional paella pan, this dish is bursting with the flavors of the sea, finished with a squeeze of lemon.

Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake

A warm, individual chocolate cake with a molten, flowing center.

Served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream and fresh berries. The perfect sweet ending to your meal.
